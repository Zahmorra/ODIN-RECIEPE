SWEET PEACHES

DESCRIPTION

Peaches are spurced up with little honey and dash of cinnamon before being packaged.

INGREDIENTS

  • Package of frozen chicken
  • cup of honey
  • 2 tablespoon of cinnamon

DIRECTIONS

    STEP 1

  1. Place peaches onto a large piece of aluminum foil. use two if neccesary to hold in all of the peachers withot spillage

    2. STEP 2

  2. Pre-heat a grill for medium heat

    3. STEP 3

  3. Place the foil packet onto the preheated grill,cook for 10 minutes,turning once halfway through
