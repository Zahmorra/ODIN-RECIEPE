CHICHEN SOUP


DESCRIPTION

Homemade chicken soup -but you dont deserve to be blue

you! deserve to enjoy it of course!. It's your right


Reciepe Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 4 carrots
  • 4 stalk chelery
  • 1 large onion
  • water to cover
  • salt and pepper
  • teaspoon

DIRECTIONS

  • STEP 1

    1. Put the chicken,carrota,celery and onion in a large soup pot and cover with cold water. Heat and simmer,uncovered,until the chicken meats fall off the bones (skim off foam every so often)

  • STEP 2

    1. Every thing out of thr pot,strain the broth. Pick the meat off the bones and chop the carrot,celery and onion. Season the broth with salt, pepper and chicken bouillon to taste, if desired.Return the chicken,carrots,celery and onion to the pot,stir together,and serve.
